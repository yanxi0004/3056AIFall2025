To assist the public in identifying scam calls and websites, the Police launched a one-stop scam and pitfall search engine ("Scameter") and an application ("Scameter+") in September 2022 and February 2023 respectively. By end-2024, it had recorded a total of 7.24 million searches and issued 940 000 alerts to the public (Figure 5). Since November 2023, the Police have also been partnering with banks to issue over 700 000 alerts to persons before they transfer money to suspicious accounts flagged in the "Scameter" database. To combat the use of stooge accounts to launder crime proceeds, the Government is exploring the feasibility of lowering the prosecution threshold for handover of bank accounts to others without reasonable due diligence beforehand. This is broadly similar to the Singaporean practice since May 2023.
