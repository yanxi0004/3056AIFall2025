"Cool Met Stuff" : Tropical Cyclone Track Probability Forecast

1 September 2017

Worrying about the likelihood of typhoon impacts during your outbound trip next week? The Observatory's new "Tropical Cyclone Track Probability Forecast" may offer you some hints. The latest episode of "Cool Met Stuff" introduces this new service, enabling easy appraisal of the trend of tropical cyclone movement in the coming nine days by members of the public through actual examples. Users are also reminded of the important notes on usage. The video is now available on the "MyObservatory" mobile application and the Observatory's website for your viewing.
