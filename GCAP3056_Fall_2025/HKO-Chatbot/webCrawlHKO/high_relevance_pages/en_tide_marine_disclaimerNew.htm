Data Source

  • Real-time tide data of Chek Lap Kok (E) and Chek Lap Kok (W) are provided by courtesy of the Airport Authority.
  • Real-time tide data of Ko Lau Wan, Kwai Chung, Ma Wan, Cheung Chau and Sha Kiu Tau are provided by the Hydrographic Office of Marine Department.
