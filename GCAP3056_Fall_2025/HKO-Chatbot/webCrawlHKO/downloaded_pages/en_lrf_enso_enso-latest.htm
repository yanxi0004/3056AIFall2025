Latest status of El Niño and La Niña

El Niño and La Niña
Latest status (September 2025)


In the past month or so, sea surface temperatures of the central and eastern equatorial Pacific cooled gradually. Based on the latest oceanic observations as well as forecasts by a number of climate models around the world, the sea surface temperatures of the central and eastern equatorial Pacific are expected to continue cooling in the next couple of months, with a possibility of briefly transitioning to La Niña conditions during the rest of this autumn and the winter 2025/26.
