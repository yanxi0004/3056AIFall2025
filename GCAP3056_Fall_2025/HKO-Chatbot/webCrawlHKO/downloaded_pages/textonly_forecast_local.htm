Bulletin updated at 11:45 HKT 06/Oct/2025

Here is the latest weather bulletin issued by the Hong Kong
Observatory.

An anticyclone aloft is bringing mainly fine weather to
southeastern China. Showers are affecting the coast of
western Guangdong.

kilometres west-southwest of Nanning. It is forecast to move
west-northwest at about 15 kilometres per hour across inland
areas of Guangxi to northern part of Vietnam and dissipate
gradually.

Sunny periods in the afternoon. Mainly cloudy tonight.
Moderate east to southeasterly winds, occasionally fresh at
first.

Outlook : Mainly fine and persistently very hot in the next
few days.
