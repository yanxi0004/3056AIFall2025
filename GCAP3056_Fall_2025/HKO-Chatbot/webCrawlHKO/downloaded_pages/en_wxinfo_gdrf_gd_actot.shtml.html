Remarks:
  1. Rainfall data for cities in Chinese Mainland is provided by the National Meteorological Centre of the China Meteorological Administration, rainfall data for Macao is provided by the Macao Meteorological and Geophysical Bureau.
  2. The rainfall data and maps are updated at around 11 a.m. each morning.
  3. The climatological mean rainfall of Hong Kong, Macao, Guangzhou, Shantou, Shanwei, Heyuan, Shaoguan, Yangjiang and Zhanjiang refers to their respective 1991-2020 climatological normals. The climatological mean rainfall of Xiamen and Haikou refers to their respective 1961-1990 climatological normals.
