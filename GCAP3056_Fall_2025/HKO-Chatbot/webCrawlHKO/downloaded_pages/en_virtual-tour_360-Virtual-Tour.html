Welcome to the 360 degree virtual tour of the Hong Kong Observatory! We will invite you to visit the attractions of the Observatory one by one, including the Headquarters in Tsim Sha Tsui and the facilities at other outstations. Some places have seldom been arranged for public visit before! You can take a 360-degree view of the surroundings and equipment. Dragging the screen left or right will show you the landscape in different directions. Clicking on the footprint icon will lead you to explore more ahead of you. When pressing the information icon , you can see a brief introduction of the set-up. The video icon will even link you to the relevant "Cool Met Stuff" video. The spots of some tours (e.g. Headquarters in Tsim Sha Tsui, Ping Chau) have been linked up. You can follow the footprint icons to go from one spot to another, or click on the map icon at the bottom to check out the other spots. Please start your journey now!
