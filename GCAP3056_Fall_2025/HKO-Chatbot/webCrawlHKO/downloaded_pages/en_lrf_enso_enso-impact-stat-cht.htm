Seasonal and annual rainfall charts

Spring - El Niño brings generally more rainfall to Hong Kong compared to the ENSO-neutral state.


Spring (March-May) rainfall of Hong Kong, 1950-2015


Summer - On average, rainfall recorded in Hong Kong during El Niño is slightly less than that under the ENSO-neutral state. However, the difference is not statistically significant.
