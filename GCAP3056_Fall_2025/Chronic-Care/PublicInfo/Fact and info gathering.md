## 1. Program Overview and Participation Statistics

### Program Launch and Objectives
- **Launch Date**: November 2023
- **Target Population**: Hong Kong residents aged 45 or above without known history of diabetes mellitus (DM) or hypertension (HT)
- **Primary Goals**: Early prevention, identification, and treatment of chronic diseases
- **Core Philosophy**: "Early prevention, early identification, early treatment"

### Current Participation Statistics (as of May 31, 2025)
- **Total Enrolled**: 131,200 individuals
- **Completed Screenings**: 74,900 participants (approximately 60% of enrolled)
- **Diagnosed with Chronic Conditions**: 31,100 participants (approximately 40% of those screened)
- **Conditions Diagnosed**: Prediabetes, DM, HT, or hyperlipidemia

## 2. Program Effectiveness and Health Outcomes

### Health Improvements
- **Diabetes Management**: Average glycosylated hemoglobin (HbA1c) reduction of approximately 1.0%
- **Hypertension Management**: Average systolic blood pressure reduction of approximately 15.0mmHg
- **Improvement Timeline**: Health improvements observed within 6 months of program participation

### Patient Satisfaction
- **Overall Program Rating**: 82.5/100 points
- **Initial Family Doctor Consultation Rating**: 76/100 points
- **Survey Participants**: 614 participants from 18 District Health Centres/Stations

### Patient Empowerment
- **Hypertension Patients**: 96.1% showed improvement in self-management capabilities
- **Diabetes Patients**: 98.0% showed improvement in self-management capabilities

## 3. Medication Policy and Drug Coverage

### Drug List Expansion
- **Original Drug List**: 59 medications
- **Expanded Drug List**: 114 medications (55 new additions)
- **Expansion Date**: August 1, 2024

### New Drug Categories Added
- Antidepressants
- Chronic Hepatitis B treatment medications
- Osteoporosis medications
- Thyroid disease medications
- Asthma and gout relief medications
- Nausea and dizziness relief medications

### Drug Subsidy Mechanism
- **Specific Drug List**: Covers over 110 essential medications
- **Family Doctor Benefits**: Access to discounted medications from designated suppliers
- **Quarterly Drug Subsidy**: Calculated based on prescription records

## 4. Financial Resources and Budget Allocation

### 2025-26 Budget Allocation
- **Healthcare Funding**: HK$137 million
- **Purpose**: Chronic Disease Co-care Pilot Scheme and Elderly Health Care Voucher optimization

### Co-payment Structure
- **Median Co-payment**: HK$150
- **Service Point Fee Changes**:
  - 90% of service points (874) maintained unchanged fees
  - 8 service points reduced fees (HK$30-150 reduction, median HK$75)
  - 89 service points increased fees (HK$20-300 increase, median HK$50)
- **Overall Fee Increase**: 3.1%

### Subsidy Details (2024-25)
- **Consultation Subsidy**: HK$266 per visit
- **Drug Subsidy**: HK$125 per quarter
- **Patient Co-payment**: HK$50 per consultation

## 5. Treatment Frequency and Service Limits

### Consultation Limits by Condition
- **Diabetes/Hypertension Patients**: Maximum 6 subsidized consultations per year
- **Prediabetes Patients**: Maximum 4 subsidized consultations per year
- **Hyperlipidemia Patients**:
  - First year: Maximum 4 subsidized consultations
  - Second year onwards: Maximum 2 subsidized consultations
- **Multiple Conditions**: Highest applicable limit applies

### Service Coverage
- **Annual Consultations**: Up to 10 subsidized consultations per year
- **Additional Services**: X-ray examinations, laboratory tests, electrocardiography (upon referral)
- **Treatment Scope**: Both chronic and episodic illnesses

## 6. Service Network Expansion and Integration

### Multi-Professional Care
- **Current Professionals**: Family doctors, nurses, allied health professionals
- **Allied Health Professionals**: Optometrists, podiatrists, nutritionists, physiotherapists
- **Proposed Expansion**: Traditional Chinese Medicine practitioners, pharmacists

### District Health Network
- **Dedicated Nurse Clinics**: Professional nursing services
- **Allied Health Services**: Multi-professional collaboration
- **Service Locations**: [District Health Network Service Locations](https://www.dhc.gov.hk/files/dedicated_nurse_clinic_and_allied_health_services/service_locations.pdf)

### GOPC Integration
- **Expansion to Public Clinics**: Services for underprivileged groups
- **Target Groups**: CSSA recipients, OALA recipients aged 75+, medical fee waiver certificate holders
- **Implementation**: Phased rollout across all 18 districts by end of 2025

## 14. Chronic Disease Statistics in Hong Kong

### Major Chronic Diseases
- **Leading Causes of Death (2020)**: Cancer, heart disease, stroke, kidney diseases, dementia, blood infections, chronic lower respiratory diseases, and diabetes
- **Percentage of Total Deaths**: Approximately 55% of all deaths in Hong Kong
- **Aging Population Impact**: Hong Kong has one of the world's fastest-aging populations

### Population Demographics
- **Aging Rate**: Average annual increase of 4.0% for population aged 65+ (2021-2030)
- **Healthcare Challenges**: Increasing prevalence of chronic diseases due to aging population
