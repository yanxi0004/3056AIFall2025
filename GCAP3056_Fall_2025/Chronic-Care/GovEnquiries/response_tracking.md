# Government Response Tracking

## Instructions for Updates
- Track all government responses
- Document key information received
- Note follow-up actions required
- Maintain timeline of communications

## Template Structure
```markdown
# Response Tracking Log

## Inquiry: [Inquiry subject]
**Date Sent**: [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Department**: [Department name]
**Response Received**: [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Status**: [Pending/Received/Follow-up Required]

### Key Information Received
- [Information point 1]
- [Information point 2]

### Follow-up Actions Required
- [ ] [Action 1]
- [ ] [Action 2]

### Next Steps
- [Next step 1]
- [Next step 2]
```
