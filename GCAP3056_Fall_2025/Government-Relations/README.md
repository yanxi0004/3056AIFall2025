# Government Relations

## Project Overview
Direct engagement with government departments for information requests, policy feedback, and collaborative initiatives.

## Project Goals
- Coordinate Freedom of Information Act requests
- Maintain government correspondence records
- Build relationships with relevant departments
- Track policy development and responses

- **reply from Census and Statistics Department.md** - Official government responses and data

2. **Census and Statistics Department** - Data requests and demographic analysis
3. **Other relevant agencies** - As projects develop

## Information Requests
