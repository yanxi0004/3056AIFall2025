Thank you for your enquiry dated 11 September 2025.  Please find below the information you requested:

As at 31.8.2025, there are eight and three EO Grade posts under the Administration Branch and the Census Planning Branch (2) of the Social Statistics Division respectively, and no AO Grade post in the Census and Statistics Department.   In the Administration Branch, their job titles (abbreviations used in the government telephone directory) are: Departmental Secretary (DS), Deputy Departmental Secretary (Dep DS), and Assistant Departmental Secretary (Asst DS).  In the Census Planning Branch (2), their job titles are: Senior Executive Officer (Census) (SEO (C)) and Executive Officer (Census) (EO (C)).

You may obtain the names of the corresponding post holders from the Government Telephone Directory (URL: [https://tel.directory.gov.hk/index_C&SD_ENG.html](https://tel.directory.gov.hk/index_C&SD_ENG.html)).
